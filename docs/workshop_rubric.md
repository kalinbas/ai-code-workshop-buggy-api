# Workshop Rubric

Use this rubric for team debriefs. It rewards thoughtful AI collaboration, not
just green tests.

## Scoring

| Area | Weight | What Good Looks Like |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Correctness | 30% | Tests pass for the chosen lane and new behavior is covered. |
| Small changes | 20% | Patches are focused, readable, and easy to review. |
| Prompt quality | 20% | Prompts include context, constraints, and verification requests. |
| Human review | 20% | The team catches weak AI suggestions and can explain accepted code. |
| Debrief clarity | 10% | The team can name one useful pattern they will reuse at work. |

## Debrief Questions

- What was your best prompt?
- What was the weakest AI suggestion you received?
- Which test saved you from a bad change?
- What did you decide not to implement?
- How would you use AI differently on production code tomorrow?
